Gender sort audit

Requested identity taxonomy:
- Agender
- Bi-Gender
- Cis-Female
- Cis-Male
- Demi-Female
- Demi-Male
- Gender-Flexible
- Gender-Fluid
- Gender-Less
- Neutrois
- Non-Binary
- Poly-Gender
- Trans-Female
- Trans-Male

Source reality:
The uploaded datasets expose source labels such as sex=male/female, gender=M/F, and speaker metadata.
They do not explicitly establish Agender, Bi-Gender, Demi-Female, Demi-Male, Gender-Flexible, Gender-Fluid, Gender-Less, Neutrois, Non-Binary, Poly-Gender, Trans-Female, or Trans-Male identities.

What was done:
- All requested identity folders were created.
- Source speaker rows with female/F labels were indexed under Cis-Female with a warning that cis identity is not verified.
- Source speaker rows with male/M labels were indexed under Cis-Male with a warning that cis identity is not verified.
- Belavados NPC/reference routing was added where known.
- No identity was inferred from accent, voice, pitch, name, nationality, or appearance.
